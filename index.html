About Me

My name is Bill Wunsch and I live in the Roscoe Village neighborhood of Chicago with my beautiful wife Lisa. We were married on June 2, 2018 on an Amish farm in Indiana. She's a video producer and a Notre Dame fan. I am not a Notre Dame fan so college football Saturday's can be fairly tense in our household.

We have two dogs, a golden retriever named Louie and a black lab mix name Bailey. They are both 9 years old and I've had them for most of their lives. They can be both lazy and have a ton of energy at the same time which can make for interesting days.
